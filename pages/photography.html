Photography

I just thought I'd have a little fun while we're here. I've been all over the world: 19 or so countries. (I hate having to count them over and over!) From 2015-2020, I spent 1 school year in southern India and a little over 3 school years on two different sides of Spain: the northwest corner that feels more like Ireland than Spain and the southeastern coast — a very Mediterranean vibe. I definitely got tired of traveling after doing so much of it and not having enough money to really enjoy it, but... I spent a lot of time taking photos of the many places I've been even for a 7-hour or 20-hour layover... both planned and un-planned!
